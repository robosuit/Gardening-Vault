## Energetics (Traditional Herbalism Framework)

### 🔥 Warming Herbs

Increase body warmth, circulation, metabolism.

- [[Anise]]
- [[Fennel]]
- [[Rosemary]]
- [[Ginger]]
- [[Black Pepper]]

**Use for:** Cold constitution, poor circulation, sluggish digestion

---

### ❄️ Cooling Herbs

Reduce heat, inflammation, fever.

- [[Peppermint]]
- [[Lemon Balm]]
- [[Chamomile]]
- [[Lavender]]

**Use for:** Inflammation, fever, heat sensitivity, irritation

---

### 💧 Moistening Herbs

Add hydration, nourish dry conditions.

- [[Lemon Balm]]
- [[Lavender]]
- [[Chamomile]]

**Use for:** Dry skin, dry mouth, dehydration

---

### 🌱 Drying Herbs

Reduce excess moisture, fluids, mucus.

- [[Sage]]
- [[Thyme]]
- [[Oregano]]
- [[White Yarrow]]

**Use for:** Damp conditions, excess mucus, sweating

---

## Combined Energetic Profiles

### Warming + Drying
- [[Rosemary]]
- [[Thyme]]
- [[Oregano]]

**Best for:** Cold, damp constitutions

### Cooling + Moistening
- [[Lavender]]
- [[Lemon Balm]]

**Best for:** Hot, dry constitutions

### Warming + Moistening
- [[Fennel]]
- [[Anise]]

**Best for:** Cold with dryness

### Cooling + Drying
- [[Sage]]
- [[White Yarrow]]

**Best for:** Heat with excess fluids
