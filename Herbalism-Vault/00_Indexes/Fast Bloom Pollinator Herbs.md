# Fast Bloom Pollinator Herbs (Greenhouse)

Target: seed-to-bloom herbs for rapid pollinator support under controlled indoor/greenhouse conditions.

- [[Calendula]]: 35-50 days (tier: Elite; indoor: Yes)
- [[Nasturtium]]: 35-50 days (tier: Elite; indoor: Yes)
- [[Cilantro]]: 40-55 days (tier: Elite; indoor: Yes)
- [[Borage]]: 45-60 days (tier: Elite; indoor: Yes)
- [[Chamomile]]: 45-60 days (tier: Elite; indoor: Yes)
- [[Dill]]: 45-60 days (tier: Elite; indoor: Yes)
- [[Marigold]]: 50-60 days (tier: Fast; indoor: Yes)
- [[Dandelion]]: 55-75 days (tier: Fast; indoor: Limited)
- [[Basil]]: 60-90 days (tier: Fast; indoor: Limited)

## 40-50 Day Priority List
- [[Calendula]] (35-50 days)
- [[Nasturtium]] (35-50 days)
- [[Cilantro]] (40-55 days, usable in the 40-50 window when bolting is encouraged)

## March Bloom Planning
- For March flowers, sow 40-60 day herbs in late January through early February under 14-16h light and stable warmth.
- Highest-priority fast options in this vault: [[Calendula]], [[Cilantro]], [[Chamomile]], [[Dill]], [[Borage]], [[Nasturtium]].
