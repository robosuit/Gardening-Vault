# 🌱 Zone 7B Herbal Planting Calendar

**Average Last Frost:** April 5–15  
**Average First Frost:** October 25–November 5

This calendar guides monthly planting and care for medicinal herb gardens in Zone 7B.

---

## 🌸 [[February]]

**Days:** Growing season begins  
**Temperature:** 35–45°F

### Start Indoors

- [[Lavender]] (slow germinator, needs 4-6 weeks)
- [[Rosemary]] (slow germinator, needs 4-6 weeks)

**Notes:** Use seed starting heat mat. Keep soil consistently moist.

---

## 🌱 [[March]]

**Days:** 8–10 hours light  
**Temperature:** 45–55°F

### Start Indoors

- [[Bergamot]]
- [[Catnip]]
- [[Echinacea]]
- [[Hyssop]]
- [[Lemon Balm]]
- [[Lovage]]
- [[Marjoram]]
- [[Mountain Mint]]
- [[Peppermint]]
- [[Lemon Mint]]
- [[Sage]]
- [[Thyme]]
- [[Oregano]]
- [[Thai Basil]]
- [[Italian Basil]]
- [[Purple Basil]]

### Direct Sow Outdoors

- [[Caraway]]
- [[Chamomile]]
- [[Cilantro]]
- [[Dandelion]]

**Notes:** Harden off indoor starts gradually before transplanting.

---

## 🌿 [[April]]

**Days:** After April 15 (after last frost)  
**Temperature:** 55–65°F

### Direct Sow

- [[Anise]]
- [[Borage]]
- [[Dill]]
- [[Fennel]]

### Transplant Outdoors

- All March indoor starts

**Notes:** Soil should be warm and workable.

---

## 🌞 [[May]]

**Days:** All frost danger passed  
**Temperature:** 65–75°F

### Succession Sow

- [[Cilantro]] (sow every 2 weeks until August)
- [[Dill]] (sow every 3 weeks)

### Warm Soil Herbs

- All [[Basil]] varieties need soil 70°F+

### Care

- Begin deadheading spent flowers
- Regular watering as temperatures rise
- Pinch basil tops for bushier growth

---

## ☀️ [[June]]

**Maintenance Month**

### Care Tasks

- Continue succession sowing cilantro & dill
- Water regularly (1-2 inches per week)
- Watch for pests on mint family plants
- Begin harvesting early perennials

### Early Harvest

- [[Chamomile]] flowers
- Basil leaves (pinch regularly)
- [[Lemon Balm]] (cut above node)

---

## 🌻 [[July]]

**Peak Growth**

### Tasks

- Monitor water during heat stress
- Harvest regularly to encourage growth
- Deadhead flowers (except for seed saving)
- Mulch around plants to retain moisture

### Harvest Window

- Most perennials at peak potency

---

## 🍂 [[August]]

**Preparation for Fall**

### Tasks

- Begin drying herbs for winter storage
- Reduce watering slightly
- Cut back basil varieties
- Last chance to direct sow cilantro

---

## 🌡️ [[September]]

**Cool Season Restart**

**Temperature:** 70–80°F

### Cool Season Herbs

- [[Cilantro]] (prefer 60-70°F)
- [[Dill]]
- [[Chamomile]]

### Care

- Reduce watering
- Begin fall cleanup of summer annuals

---

## 🍁 [[October]]

**Season's End Approach**

### Tasks

- Monitor frost dates (Oct 25–Nov 5)
- Protect tender herbs with frost cloth
- Harvest heavily before frost
- Prepare perennials for dormancy

---

## ❄️ [[November - January]]

**Winter Dormancy**

### Perennials

- Mulch around base (2-3 inches) in late November
- Reduce watering to minimum
- No fertilizer
- Clean up dead annuals

### Indoor Care

- Start seeds indoors in February
- Plan next year (late December)
- Order seeds ahead of spring rush
