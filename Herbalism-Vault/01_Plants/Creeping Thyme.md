# Creeping Thyme

## Growing Conditions
Prefers Full sun with well-drained soil and steady moisture.

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
Start: Spring under lights or protected greenhouse conditions.
See [[Month-by-Month Calendar (Zone 7B)]] for timing details.

## Seed Starting and Bloom Timing
- Start method: Start indoors 8-10 weeks before transplanting.
- Days to germination: 10-21 days
- Bloom from seed: 70-100 days
- Bloom speed tier: Moderate
- Indoor bloom viability: Limited
