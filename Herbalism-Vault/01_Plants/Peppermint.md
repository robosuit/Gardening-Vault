# Peppermint

## Growing Conditions
Prefers Full sun to partial shade (shade preferred in deep summer) conditions, with Average to rich, moist (loves water) soil and Moist (consistent; tolerates wet better than dry) moisture.

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
Start: March-April (transplant) or divide spring/fall
Harvest: June-October (continuous harvest possible)
