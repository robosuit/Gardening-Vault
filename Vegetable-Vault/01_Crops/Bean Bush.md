# Bean Bush

## Quick Use Guide
- Seed depth: 1-1.5 in
- Spacing: rows 24-36 in, plants 2-3 in
- Feeding level: Nitrogen Fixer
- Rotation note: Good crop before heavy feeders

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
- Spring sow/plant window: Apr 15-Jul 15
- Fall sow/plant window: Jul 10-Aug 1
- Outdoor transplant window: Usually direct sow only
- Indoor start lead time: Direct sow after soil warms
- Frost baseline used: last spring frost Apr 5-Apr 15; first fall frost Oct 25-Nov 5.

## Seed Depth and Spacing
- Seed/planting depth: 1-1.5 in
- Row spacing: 24-36 in
- In-row spacing: 2-3 in
- Timing windows verified against Virginia Cooperative Extension (Table 3) and Clemson Extension planting chart.

## Transplanting Time and Procedure
- Direct sow/planting is the standard method for this crop.
- Prepare a fine seedbed and keep topsoil evenly moist through germination.
- Thin seedlings early to final spacing to reduce disease pressure.
