# Daikon Radish

## Quick Use Guide
- Seed depth: 1/2 in
- Spacing: rows 12-18 in, plants 3-4 in
- Feeding level: Light
- Companion group: Root

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
- Start window: Mar-Apr and Aug-Sep
- Indoor start method: Direct sow or indoor tray sowing for rapid bolting.
- Harvest window: Apr-May and Oct-Nov

## Seed Starting and Bloom Timing
- Days to germination: 3-7 days
- Bloom from seed: 40-55 days
- Bloom speed tier: Elite
- Indoor bloom viability: Yes

## Varieties
- Miyashige: classic white long-root type
- Minowase: large long storage type
- KN Bravo: smooth roots with strong uniformity

## Crop Notes
Great for compaction relief and bio-drilling when roots are left to decompose.
