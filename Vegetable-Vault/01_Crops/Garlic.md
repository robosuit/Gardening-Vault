# Garlic

## Quick Use Guide
- Seed depth: 1-2 in (cloves)
- Spacing: rows 6-12 in, plants 3-6 in
- Feeding level: Medium
- Rotation note: Good disease-break crop between heavy feeders

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
- Fall sow/plant window: Sep 15-Nov 15
- Outdoor transplant window: Plant cloves in fall
- Indoor start lead time: No indoor start needed
- Frost baseline used: last spring frost Apr 5-Apr 15; first fall frost Oct 25-Nov 5.

## Seed Depth and Spacing
- Seed/planting depth: 1-2 in (cloves)
- Row spacing: 6-12 in
- In-row spacing: 3-6 in
- Timing windows verified against Virginia Cooperative Extension (Table 3) and Clemson Extension planting chart.

## Transplanting Time and Procedure
- Direct sow/planting is the standard method for this crop.
- Prepare a fine seedbed and keep topsoil evenly moist through germination.
- Thin seedlings early to final spacing to reduce disease pressure.

- Music: hardy hardneck with large cloves

- Chesnok Red: flavorful purple-striped hardneck

## Crop Notes
Mulch heavily after planting for winter protection.
