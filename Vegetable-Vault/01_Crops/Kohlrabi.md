# Kohlrabi

## Quick Use Guide
- Seed depth: 1/4-1/2 in seed; transplants about 3 in deep
- Spacing: rows 24 in, plants 6-8 in
- Feeding level: Medium
- Rotation note: Short-season brassica for rotation gaps

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
- Spring sow/plant window: Feb 1-Mar 15
- Fall sow/plant window: Aug 15-Sep 30
- Outdoor transplant window: Early spring and late summer
- Indoor start lead time: Start 4-6 weeks before transplanting
- Frost baseline used: last spring frost Apr 5-Apr 15; first fall frost Oct 25-Nov 5.

## Seed Depth and Spacing
- Seed/planting depth: 1/4-1/2 in seed; transplants about 3 in deep
- Row spacing: 24 in
- In-row spacing: 6-8 in
- Timing windows verified against Virginia Cooperative Extension (Table 3) and Clemson Extension planting chart.

## Transplanting Time and Procedure
- Direct sow is effective once soil is warm enough.
- Optional transplanting can speed harvest by 1-2 weeks.
- Harden off starts for 7-10 days and avoid root disturbance.
- Water in deeply after planting and keep moisture steady until new growth.

## Crop Notes
