# Leek

## Quick Use Guide
- Seed depth: 1/4 in seed; set transplants about 3 in deep
- Spacing: rows 24 in, plants 3-4 in
- Feeding level: Medium
- Rotation note: Moderate feeder; good before root crops

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
- Spring sow/plant window: Jan 15-Feb 15 (start plants)
- Fall sow/plant window: Jul 1-Aug 15 (start for fall)
- Outdoor transplant window: Mar-Apr and Aug-Sep
- Indoor start lead time: Start 8-10 weeks before transplanting
- Frost baseline used: last spring frost Apr 5-Apr 15; first fall frost Oct 25-Nov 5.

## Seed Depth and Spacing
- Seed/planting depth: 1/4 in seed; set transplants about 3 in deep
- Row spacing: 24 in
- In-row spacing: 3-4 in
- Timing windows verified against Virginia Cooperative Extension (Table 3) and Clemson Extension planting chart.

## Transplanting Time and Procedure
- Best method: transplant starts/slips/crowns in the window above.
- Harden off starts for 7-10 days before planting outdoors.
- Transplant in late afternoon or on cloudy days to reduce shock.
- Water in thoroughly and mulch after soil has warmed.
