# Luffa

## Quick Use Guide
- Seed depth: 1 in
- Spacing: rows 60-72 in, plants 18-24 in
- Feeding level: Heavy
- Companion group: Cucurbit

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
- Start window: Apr-May (indoors start in Mar-Apr)
- Indoor start method: Start indoors 4-6 weeks before transplanting.
- Harvest window: Aug-Oct

## Seed Starting and Bloom Timing
- Days to germination: 7-14 days
- Bloom from seed: 55-75 days
- Bloom speed tier: Fast
- Indoor bloom viability: Limited

## Varieties
- Smooth Luffa: long, straight sponge gourds
- Angled Luffa: ridged fruit, often earlier

## Crop Notes
Use trellising for straight fruit and clean sponge quality.
