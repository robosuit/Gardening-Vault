# Parsley

## Quick Use Guide
- Seed depth: 1/4-1/2 in
- Spacing: rows 12-24 in, plants 3-6 in
- Feeding level: Light
- Rotation note: Light feeder useful as bed-edge crop

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
- Spring sow/plant window: Mar 15-Apr 15
- Fall sow/plant window: Jul 15-Aug 1
- Outdoor transplant window: Mar-Apr and Aug
- Indoor start lead time: Start 6-8 weeks before transplanting
- Frost baseline used: last spring frost Apr 5-Apr 15; first fall frost Oct 25-Nov 5.

## Seed Depth and Spacing
- Seed/planting depth: 1/4-1/2 in
- Row spacing: 12-24 in
- In-row spacing: 3-6 in
- Timing windows verified against Virginia Cooperative Extension (Table 3) and Clemson Extension planting chart.

## Transplanting Time and Procedure
- Direct sow is effective once soil is warm enough.
- Optional transplanting can speed harvest by 1-2 weeks.
- Harden off starts for 7-10 days and avoid root disturbance.
- Water in deeply after planting and keep moisture steady until new growth.

## Avoid Planting Near
- Crowded blocks with poor airflow
- Hot, dry, reflected-heat beds

## Varieties
- Flat-leaf Italian: strong culinary flavor
- Curled: decorative dense foliage
- Hamburg: grown for edible root and tops

## Crop Notes
Soak seed overnight to speed germination.
