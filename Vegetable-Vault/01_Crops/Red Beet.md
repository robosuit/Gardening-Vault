# Red Beet

## Quick Use Guide
- Seed depth: 1/2 in
- Spacing: rows 12-18 in, plants 2-3 in
- Feeding level: Light
- Companion group: Root

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
- Start window: Mar-Apr and Jul-Aug
- Indoor start method: Direct sow or cell trays; transplant when small.
- Harvest window: May-Jun and Sep-Nov

## Seed Starting and Bloom Timing
- Days to germination: 5-10 days
- Bloom from seed: 50-70 days
- Bloom speed tier: Fast
- Indoor bloom viability: Yes

## Varieties
- Detroit Dark Red: classic table beet
- Red Ace: uniform sweet roots
- Bull's Blood: deep red roots and leaves

## Crop Notes
Useful for root production and for managed bolting to support early pollinators.
