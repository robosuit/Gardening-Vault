# Sugarcane

## Quick Use Guide
- Seed depth: 2-4 in (setts)
- Spacing: rows 48-60 in, plants 18-24 in
- Feeding level: Heavy
- Companion group: Grass

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
- Start window: Mar-May (protected warmth)
- Indoor start method: Plant cane cuttings in warm controlled environment.
- Harvest window: Late fall through winter in warm systems

## Seed Starting and Bloom Timing
- Days to germination: 14-30 days
- Bloom from seed: 300+ days
- Bloom speed tier: Slow
- Indoor bloom viability: No
