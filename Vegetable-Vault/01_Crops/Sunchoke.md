# Sunchoke

## Quick Use Guide
- Seed depth: 4-6 in (tubers)
- Spacing: rows 36-42 in, plants 12-18 in
- Feeding level: Medium
- Companion group: Root

## Planting Calendar (Zone 7B)
- Start window: Mar-Apr (tubers)
- Indoor start method: Plant tubers directly; seed is uncommon for home systems.
- Harvest window: Oct-Dec

## Seed Starting and Bloom Timing
- Days to germination: 10-21 days
- Bloom from seed: 120-160 days
- Bloom speed tier: Slow
- Indoor bloom viability: No

## Varieties
- Fuseau: smooth, elongated tubers
- Stampede: productive, uniform stand
- Red Fuseau: red-skinned elongated tubers

## Crop Notes
Also called Jerusalem artichoke; tall late-season pollinator support flowers.
